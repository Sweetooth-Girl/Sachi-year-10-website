The Adorable Duo.

The Adorable Duo(Aduo for short) is a duo that represent the surface impression of Sweettooth. The best way to describe them is "right brain and left brain". They're essentially the Protagonists of MSG and one of the first SGs Sweettooth ever made.

Prim is the 'creative' and 'energetic' SG with a curiosity of a cat and the survival instinct of a toast. She uses the sciency water gun called H20 gun that shoots out one of the primary color of Prim's choice.

Arch is the 'logical' and 'strategic' SG, organising thoughts and ideas to excute yet having an accuracy of a water bottle flip. Her entire life is just try and error. She holds arched bow and special arrows that when lit by one of the Prim's colors, It gains elemental attribute based off that color(red = fire, blue = water, and yellow is uh... yellow).
