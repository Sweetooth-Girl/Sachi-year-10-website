The Adorable Duo.

About the duo:

The Adorable Duo(Aduo for short) is a duo that represent the surface impression of Sweettooth. The best way to describe them is "right brain and left brain". They're essentially the Protagonists of MSG and one of the first SGs Sweettooth ever made.

About Prim:

Prim is the 'creative' and 'energetic' SG. Having a curiosity of a cat and the survival instinct of a toast.

About Arch:

Arch is the 'logical' and 'strategic' SG. organizes thoughts and ideas to excute yet having an accuracy of a water bottle flip. Her entire life is just try and error.
