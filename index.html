What/Who is SG Anyways?

SGs are fictional species/Original characters made and drawn by the creator Sweettooth. Each SGs are meant to represent characteristics of Sweettooth but each has their own personality and thoughts.

But then who is Sweettooth?

Sweettooth(SweettoothGirl) is a delusional artist who wish to become famous from her creative works. 99% of things in this website is created by her.
